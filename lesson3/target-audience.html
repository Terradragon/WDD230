Target Audience

This site will be available to everyone around the world at mainly around the age of 12 and up. Accesability will be friendly for both desktop users as well as mobile users.


Personas

The personas listed are fictional, but give an idea of where the information for the site is gathered.


Name:

John Doe


Occupation:

He is a veterinarian specifically working with farm animals. He goes out on calls often and likes to prepare for any possible outcomes when it comes to his job and the weather.

Demographics:

40

Single

Goal(s):

To spend his evenings riding his horse and caring for the stable he owns; he also hopes to one day find a future spouse that enjoys the outdoors and farm life as much as he does.


Quote(s):

"There is no on call appoinment too big to physically handle. I hope that my desire to help care for these animals is a lesson to others on how to show compassion and empathy for everything around us."



Name:

Mary Sue Williams



Occupation:

She is a stay-at-home mother studying online at the BYUIdaho Pathway Program to obtain an education alongside her two children.

Demographics:

26

Married

2 children


Goal(s):

To go on a morning jog every day; to find the time to spend with her family as much as she possibly can; to be able to enjoy the outdoors while she is studying for school.


Quote(s):

"The more we understand, the better we can prepare. I hope to teach my children this as they continue to grow."
