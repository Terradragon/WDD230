White Space and Clean Design

Apple
https://www.apple.com/

Sometimes – when you are trying to announce or describe something – less is more. The picture within the image alone with a few words to describe it is enough to tell the viewer about the product the company is selling.

PARC: Proximity

Betty Crocker
https://www.bettycrocker.com/

The piece of the site shown here shows different recipes for each day of the week. This group is placed close into a group to keep it separate from everything else within the site.
