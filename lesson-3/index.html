Site Name


The name of this site will be called "Weather Here or There"


Site Purpose


The purpose of this site is to show the weather not only for the present day, but also for the next week to come. It will also have the option of seeing the weather in different parts of the world, whether it be in the staes, or in a different country. It will also give warnings of possible severe storm, pollen count, humidity, etc.
